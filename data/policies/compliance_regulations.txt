REGULATORY COMPLIANCE FRAMEWORK FOR FINANCIAL TRANSACTIONS

1. KNOW YOUR CUSTOMER (KYC) REQUIREMENTS
- All users must complete identity verification before high-value transactions
- Enhanced Due Diligence (EDD) required for Politically Exposed Persons (PEPs)
- Customer risk profiles must be updated quarterly
- Ongoing monitoring of customer transaction behavior against established profiles
- Documentation requirements: Government ID, proof of address, source of funds

2. ANTI-MONEY LAUNDERING (AML) PROTOCOLS
- Suspicious Activity Reports (SARs) must be filed within 30 days of detection
- Currency Transaction Reports (CTRs) for transactions exceeding $10,000
- Structuring detection: Monitor for split transactions designed to avoid reporting thresholds
- Record retention: All transaction records must be maintained for minimum 5 years
- Cross-border wire transfers require additional screening and documentation

3. PAYMENT CARD INDUSTRY (PCI) STANDARDS
- Card data must be encrypted in transit and at rest using AES-256
- Real-time fraud monitoring is mandatory for all card-present and card-not-present transactions
- Chargebacks exceeding 1% trigger enhanced monitoring requirements
- Tokenization required for stored card credentials
- Regular security assessments and penetration testing mandatory

4. REAL-TIME MONITORING REQUIREMENTS
- All transactions must be screened within 100 milliseconds of initiation
- High-risk transactions require secondary verification within 500 milliseconds
- Alert escalation: Unresolved alerts must be escalated within 15 minutes
- Audit trail: Complete transaction lifecycle must be logged with timestamps
- System availability: Fraud detection must maintain 99.99% uptime

5. DATA PROTECTION AND PRIVACY
- Customer financial data classified as PII - GDPR and CCPA compliance required
- Data minimization: Only collect necessary transaction attributes
- Right to explanation: Customers can request explanation for flagged transactions
- Cross-border data transfers require explicit compliance verification
- Quantum-processed data must maintain equivalent encryption standards
