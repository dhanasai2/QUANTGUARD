QUANTGUARD FINANCIAL FRAUD DETECTION POLICY v2.1

1. TRANSACTION MONITORING THRESHOLDS
- Standard transactions: $0 - $2,000 (Low Risk)
- Elevated transactions: $2,000 - $4,000 (Medium Risk)
- High-value transactions: $4,000+ (High Risk - requires enhanced verification)
- Micro-transaction clusters: 10+ transactions under $50 within 1 hour (Potential structuring)

2. GEOGRAPHIC ANOMALY RULES
- Transactions from different countries within 2 hours: Flag for review
- First-time transactions from high-risk jurisdictions: Enhanced due diligence required
- Location mismatch with user registered address: Medium risk flag
- Impossible travel: Transactions from distant cities within short time frames

3. VELOCITY CHECKS
- More than 5 transactions per user per hour: Velocity alert
- Transaction amount exceeding 2.5x rolling 24-hour average: Spike alert
- Consecutive declined transactions followed by approval: Potential card testing
- Rapid-fire small transactions: Potential structuring or bot activity

4. CATEGORY-SPECIFIC RULES
- Electronics purchases over $3,000: Enhanced verification required
- Travel bookings from new locations: Geographic risk assessment
- Healthcare transactions at unusual hours: Temporal anomaly flag
- Entertainment spending spikes: Behavioral deviation alert
- Cross-category rapid spending: Profile deviation flag

5. QUANTUM RISK SCORING THRESHOLDS
- Quantum fraud probability above 0.70: CRITICAL - Immediate transaction hold
- Quantum fraud probability 0.45-0.70: HIGH - Enhanced monitoring and analyst review
- Quantum fraud probability 0.25-0.45: MEDIUM - Flagged for pattern analysis
- Quantum fraud probability below 0.25: LOW - Standard processing continues

6. PATTERN RECOGNITION
- Account takeover indicators: Password change followed by high-value transaction
- Synthetic identity fraud: New account with immediate high spending
- Bust-out fraud: Gradual credit increase followed by maximum utilization
- Money mule activity: Rapid fund transfers across multiple accounts
